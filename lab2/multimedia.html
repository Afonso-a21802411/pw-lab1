Bangkok

C.K.Krieger

Skyscrapers protruding from tin rooftops
Canals of mud and flowers
Running in circles around the city
Magnificent temples of gold and jade
Alongside houses of fallen dreams
Streets of young women selling souls
Foreigners looking for companionship
And me stupidly thinking I am young
With Values too old to compromise
